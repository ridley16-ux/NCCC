About No Context Cinema Club

No Context Cinema Club, a show where an almost unbelievably unqualified person (Rob) rewrites film history by creating plots based only on titles and some (usually) unhelpful clues that Kev provides him about the real films. Each week we'll explore Rob's complete ignorance of film history, testing the limits of what is possible to have missed from pop culture during his 30-something years on this planet, and hopefully having some fun along the way.
