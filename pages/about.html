About No Context Cinema Club

No Context Cinema Club is the podcast where classic films are boldly rewritten by someone who hasn’t seen them.

Each week, Rob builds an entirely new plot from the title alone, while Kev drip-feeds clues designed to help - but usually don’t. No research. No Googling. No takebacks.

What follows: confident chaos, accidental parallels, unnecessary montages, and a final score for coherence, originality and entertainment.

Meet the hosts

Host

Rob

Host

What to expect

  • Weekly episodes built on one simple rule: no research, no takebacks
  • Rob rewriting iconic films from the title alone
  • Strategic clues from Kev that somehow make things worse

  • Very few spoilers for the real film (usually)
